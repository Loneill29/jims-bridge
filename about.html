OUR MISSION


The purpose of The James F. Mikulski Memorial Group, Inc is to establish a legacy of sharing, caring and helping others in memory of James F. Mikulski, a wonderful and loving husband, step-father, son, brother, family member and friend who suddenly passed away on October 2, 2013.

The organization will accomplish its purpose through sponsored activities and fund-raising promoting wellness, service and the opportunity to support the education of needy school aged children. The organization is entirely run by volunteers. The beneficiaries of donations received through these activities will be certified grade school educational institutions in the Chicago area.

HOW WE HELP


The James F. Mikulski Memorial Group, Inc will be funded through private donations, online contributions and donations in recognition of volunteer services.
