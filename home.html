ABOUT JIM


We are saddened that our wonderful, loving son, Jim, passed away unexpectedly on October 2, 2013 in California. He was an incredible son, brother, husband, step-father and friend.

While we miss Jim tremendously, we will always treasure the time and many wonderful memories that we had with him. We have established this foundation in his memory.


OUR MISSION


To make a difference in the lives of children in the Chicago area and establish a legacy of sharing, caring and helping others in memory of James F. Mikulski. We accomplish this through sponsored activities and fund-raising promoting wellness, service and the opportunity to support the education of needy school-aged children.
