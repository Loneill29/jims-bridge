LENDING A HAND


Volunteering is one of the cornerstones for Jim’s Bridge. In many cases, the volunteering organizations will make a donation to Jim’s Bridge in appreciation of our work. Their contributions go directly to help our beneficiaries. Volunteering at local races also helps to support runners’ and walkers’ healthy lifestyles.


However, did you know that there is research which shows that volunteering helps the well-being of the volunteers themselves? Read up on how volunteering may make people happier as well as how volunteering may be good for the body and the mind.
