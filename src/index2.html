About Jim

We are saddened that our wonderful, loving son, Jim, passed away unexpectedly on October 2, 2013 in California. He was an incredible son, brother, husband, step-father and friend. While we miss Jim tremendously, we will always treasure the time and many wonderful memories that we had with him. In his memory, we are establishing a legacy of giving, starting with a memorial scholarship fund in his name to assist the students of needy grade schools in the Chicago area. Donations for this fund can be given directly through this website.

Our Mission

To make a difference in the lives of children in the Chicago area and establish a legacy of sharing, caring and helping others in memory of James F. Mikulski.
