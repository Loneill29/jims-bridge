THE HOT CHOCOLATE 15K/5K


SUNDAY OCTOBER 30, 2016

The Hot Chocolate 5k/15k started and ended at Grant Park in downtown Chicago. A total of 43 family and friends came together at 6:00am that brisk autumn morning as Runner Information Volunteers, providing information and direction to race participants and spectators. Additionally, key volunteers from our team provided oversight and coordination within the seven runner information zones throughout Grant Park and on Michigan Avenue.

CHICAGO MARATHON


SUNDAY OCTOBER 9, 2016

The Bank of America Chicago Marathon started and ended around Grant Park in downtown Chicago. Two shifts of family and friends came together on the Lakefront Path that brisk morning as "Info Team" volunteers to provide information to runners and spectators along Michigan Avenue before and during the race. First shift arrived at 4:30am while second shift arrived at 9:30am.

TRANSAMERICA CHICAGO TRIATHLON


SUNDAY AUGUST 28, 2016

The Transamerica Chicago Triathlon started and ended around Grant Park in downtown Chicago. Two shifts of family and friends came together on the Lakefront Path at 31st Street that sunny and warm morning as "Aid Station E" volunteers providing water, "Skratch" power drink, and energetic support for the triathletes.
