HOT CHOCOLATE 5K/15K


SUNDAY NOVEMBER 8, 2015

The Hot Chocolate 5k/15k started and ended at Grant Park in downtown Chicago. A total of 53 family and friends came together at 5:00am that brisk autumn morning as Runner Information Volunteers, providing information and direction to race participants and spectators. Additionally, key volunteers from our team provided oversight and coordination within the seven runner information zones throughout Grant Park and on Michigan Avenue.

CHICAGO MARATHON


SUNDAY OCTOBER 11, 2015

The Bank of America Chicago Marathon started and ended in downtown Chicago around Grant Park. A total of 80 family and friends came together that morning to raise funds for The James F. Mikulski Memorial Group by way of providing information to runners and spectators along Michigan Avenue before and during the race. First shift arrived at 4:30am while second shift arrived at 9:30am.

SHAMROCK SHUFFLE EXPO


SATURDAY MARCH 28, 2015

The participants of the 2015 Bank of America Shamrock Shuffle 8k were asked to pick up their race bib and timing chip at the expo being held in McCormick Place. A total of 12 family and friends came together at 8:30am and worked a three hour shift greeting the runners while handing out their race bibs and timing chips.
