THE HOT CHOCOLATE 15K/5K


SUNDAY OCTOBER 29, 2017

The Hot Chocolate 5k/15k started and ended at Grant Park in downtown Chicago. A total of 44 family and friends came together at 5:30am that brisk autumn morning as Runner Information Volunteers, providing information and direction to race participants and spectators. Additionally, key volunteers from our team provided oversight and coordination within the seven runner information zones throughout Grant Park and on Michigan Avenue.
